Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals

What is it?

Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals is a module that equips students with core knowledge and skills in digital fabrication, such as computer aided design, 2D/3D modelling, 3D printing, laser-cutting, electronics and embedded programming. These skills are developed through a range of hands-on, real-world activities.
