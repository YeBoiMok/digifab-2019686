Final Project

Laser Cutting a Music Box


I first sketched the different sides of the box and ensured that the dimensions would fit the music box.


I then extruded out all the sides of it by 3mm. After that, i exported all the sketches as .dxf files.


Transferred the .dxf to .svg file in Inkscape and added designs, but sadly ended up not using it as it was not to scale.
